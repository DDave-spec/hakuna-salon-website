The Hakuna Philosophy

Hakuna means "No Trouble." It means peace, calm, and an unshakeable confidence in who you are. We built this brand on one belief: every Nigerian woman deserves to wear a crown that truly reflects her power.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does a wig installation take? expand_more
A standard wig installation takes 1–2 hours depending on the method (glue, tape, or sew-in). Customization like bleaching and plucking can add another 30–60 minutes.
Do you offer same-day delivery in Abuja? expand_more
Yes! Orders placed before 12pm Monday–Saturday qualify for same-day delivery within Abuja. A small delivery fee applies based on your location.
What's the difference between closure and frontal wigs? expand_more
A closure covers a 4×4 or 5×5 area at the top of the head, while a frontal spans the entire hairline from ear to ear (13×4 or 13×6). Frontals allow more versatile styling including side-parting and updos.
How do I care for my Hakuna wig? expand_more
We recommend washing with sulfate-free shampoo every 2–4 weeks, air-drying, and storing on a wig stand. Our stylists provide a personalized care guide with every purchase. Avoid excessive heat without a heat protectant.
Can I return or exchange a wig? expand_more
Unworn and uncut wigs in original packaging can be returned within 7 days for store credit or exchange. Once a wig has been installed or cut, it cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.
